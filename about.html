About Us

The seeds for Blueberry County Hospital were planted in 1891, when John Blueberry established Blueberry Hospital, now known as Blueberry County Hospital, in Blueberry, Washington. Over the years, our health care ministry has grown with the enduring goal of fulfilling the emotional and physical needs of the people we serve.
