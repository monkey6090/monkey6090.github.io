A mission to heal, a promise to care
Our physicians and team members create healthier communities through healing the whole person — in mind, body and spirit.
Our Mission
The mission of Blueberry County Hospital is to nurture the healing ministry of the County, supported by education and research. We focus and emphasize on human dignity and social justice as we create healthier communities.
Our Vision
We will lead the transformation of health care to achieve optimal health and wellbeing for the individuals and communities we serve, especially those who are poor and vulnerable.
Our Values
Reverence
Profound respect and awe for all creation, the foundation that shapes spirituality, our relationships with others and our journey to God
Integrity
Moral wholeness, soundness, fidelity, trust and truthfulness in all we do
Compassion
Solidarity with one another and the capacity to enter into another’s joy and sorrow
Excellence
Achieving preeminent performance, becoming the benchmark and putting forth our personal and professional best

Local News: Hospital Heroes wraps up, surpasses meal donation goal

An initiative lead by staff at Blueberry County Hospital that fed local hospital workers wrapped up this past weekend.
Hospital Heroes handed out 11,300 meals to doctors, nurses, and everyone else working at local hospitals during the current pandemic.
Local restaurants and businesses provided food and money for the program.
Meals were made up of anything you could imagine, like donuts and coffee for breakfast, and pasta dishes for dinner.

Local News: Coronavirus pandemic forces the funeral industry online


If you know someone who’s had to plan a funeral during the Covid-19 shutdown, you realize how much tougher it is to go through this already difficult task.
Most states have limited gatherings to no more than 10 people. Planning and pricing a funeral online is a service the industry has not usually provided.
“The funeral industry is very much steeped in tradition,” said Chris Cruger, chief operating officer at The Foresight Companies, a consulting firm for the industry.
